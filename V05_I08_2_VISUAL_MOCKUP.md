## Color Scheme

### Light Mode
- **Background:** White / Slate-50
- **Text:** Slate-900 (headings), Slate-700 (body)
- **Cards:** White with subtle shadow
- **Borders:** Slate-200
- **Primary Button:** Blue-600 (hover: Blue-700)
- **Secondary Button:** Slate-200 (hover: Slate-300)
- **Success:** Green-50 background, Green-600 text
- **Error:** Red-50 background, Red-600 text
- **Red Flags Badge:** Red-600 background, white text
- **Icons:** Slate-600

### Dark Mode
- **Background:** Slate-900
- **Text:** Slate-50 (headings), Slate-300 (body)
- **Cards:** Slate-800 with subtle shadow
- **Borders:** Slate-700
- **Primary Button:** Blue-500 (hover: Blue-600)
- **Secondary Button:** Slate-700 (hover: Slate-600)
- **Success:** Green-900/20 background, Green-100 text
- **Error:** Red-900/20 background, Red-100 text
- **Red Flags Badge:** Red-900/30 background, Red-400 text
- **Icons:** Slate-400
